About the Fan Page

Welcome to the unofficial fan page dedicated to the Department of Basic Education of the Republic of South Africa! This page is created by passionate individuals who admire the department's commitment to providing quality education and fostering a brighter future for the nation.

This fan page is not an official representation of the Department of Basic Education but a space where enthusiasts can come together to appreciate and discuss its initiatives and contributions to the education sector.
